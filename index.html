Fully Responsive

Responsive web design makes web pages render properly on a wide variety of device screen sizes without cutting short or distorting the content. This doesn't mean a website will appear exactly the same way on a phone as on a laptop, for example. Rather, it means that the content of the web page adapts to different screen sizes – from large screens (desktops and laptops) to medium screens (tablets) to mobile screens (phones of various screen sizes).
